The Wonderful World of Wes Anderson Filmography

Wes Anderson is an American filmmaker originally from Texas who has captivated global audiences with his distinct film-style that includes 90 degree angle camera shots, unique color schemes, and interesting fonts all wrapped into often beautiful, quirky, and humorous stories. This map is tracing all Wes Anderson movies that he has directed, produced, or written including the estimated movie budgets in USD. delineated through proportional symbols.
